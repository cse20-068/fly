WeFlyHigh Booking Agency 

ABOUT US

WeFlyHigh booking agency is an aviation company that was launched by a young female by the name of Koketso Rakwadi, a professional Aerospace Project Manager. It was first launched in Gaborone, Botswana in September 2019. Black-tie was then branched out to three other local locations namely Kasane, Francistown and Maun respectively.

This company aims to ease the booking process for each potential passenger or customer. WeFlyHigh guarantees lucid, easy, secure and good quality services offered every day of the week at the comfort of your home. 

WeFlyHigh agency provides booking services to destinations such as South Africa, Cyprus, Portugal, Netherlands and Russia, just to name a few. Upon the completion of the flight booking process, an E-ticket (electronic ticket) will be awarded to the passenger through a personal confirmation email. The information provided by the WeFlyHigh agency system will be needed and necessary on the departure day in order to initialize any flight processes thereof. 

  This company prides itself with the large number of employees that work extra hard to provide the best services any customer can have. When this agency was first launched, there were seven employees, but as of now, there are fifty-five hard working employees.

2.Registration Of Flight

For users to register flights, they have to search for flight locations (departure and arrival destinations) as well as whether they prefer a round trip or a one way. Users should also be allowed to pick whether they wish to have a seat along the isle of by the window, middle seat or they should be picked for randomly.  Users will be provided with a field where they specify the Name and requested details of the ticket holder. HOWEVER if a user is booking for;

a).A user whose identity doesn’t match that of the WEFLYHIGH account owner OR

b).They are booking for more than 2 people, they should book 72 hours in advance and provide VALID national identity documents for each of the other mentioned parties. The number of tickets bought will be equal to the number of fields presented for to which email the ticket should be sent to.

At checkout, Users will be requested to perform and verify a Direct Pay Online transaction.

3.Login

This is done by use of the email address or phone number that the user used to register their account. A field will be provided for email address or phone number AND password set for the account. Below the login field will be a register button will lead a user to create an account.
